About Me

Gillian Coppola is an artist, writer, and musician from Brooklyn, New York. She is a LaGuardia High School graduate and will be pursuing a degree in Illustration at Pratt Institute. Her art has appeared in shows throughout her time in high school, including a dedicated Senior Show.

Her writing has earned her two Gold Keys and a Silver Key in Scholastic Art & Writing competitions, and her short story "Live" won second place in the Brooklyn Public Library's Ned Vizzini Writing Contest.Her writing has earned her two Gold Keys and a Silver Key in Scholastic Art & Writing competitions, and her short story "Live" won second place in the Brooklyn Public Library's Ned Vizzini Writing Contest.

Gillian began teaching herself guitar at 17, then proceeded to get professional lessons and has since played at one recital and at the opening of an art show. She continues to practice every day and expand her breadth of songs as well as try new things with her songwriting in guitar and lyrics.
