Here you'll find my art portfolio from over the years, including sculpture, painting, prints, and more; I have explored each during my time at F.H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts.

You'll also find my writing portfolio, from short stories to poems to songs. Many of them have been published or have won awards with organizations such as Scholastic. Some of the short stories are affiliated with my two upcoming books, The Downward and The Escoffier Country House.

Lastly, you'll find a collection of videos of my guitar performances. I have been playing for a year and a half and I enjoy writing music as well as playing a range of classic rock songs.
